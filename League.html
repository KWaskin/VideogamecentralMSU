New League of Legends Champion

The new League of Legends champion Viego is being released on patch 11.2. Below is a description of what his attacks will be. He is a jungler and a top laner. His passive "Sovereign's  Domination"  allows him to posses enemies where he heals for a portion of his max health. He also gets bonus movement speed when attacking. His "Q" "Blade of the Ruined King"  uses his sword stabbing forward.  The second stab steal health instead of dealing damage. His "W" "Spectral Maw" allows Viego to dash forward and stun and do damage to the enemy.      

Viego's "E" "Harrowed Path" allows him to create a mist where inside he is camoflauged and gains attack speed and movement speed. His "R" "Heartbreaker" allows him to discard bodies he's possessing and teleports forward, attacking the enemy with the lowest health dealing damage.

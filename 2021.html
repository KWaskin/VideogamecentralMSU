Games Coming in 2021 

Pokemon Snap is a recreation of the popular game originally released on the Nintendo 64. It will be releason on April 30, 2021. The player travels around unknown islands where they take images of pokemon and try to fill out their Photodex. It will be exclusively on the Nintendo Switch. Info from https://www.nintendo.com/games/detail/new-pokemon-snap-switch/Image from https://www.nintendo.com/games/detail/new-pokemon-snap-switch/

The Kingdom Hearts franchise is one of the most popular video game franchises of all time. it is finally being remade for PC. The game follows characters teaming up with Disney characters traveling to their worlds to bring down the forces of darkness. Info from https://www.polygon.com/2021/2/11/22278635/kingdom-hearts-pc-release-date-epic-games-storeImage from https://hypebeast.com/2020/2/kingdom-hearts-hd-1-5-2-5-remix-kingdom-hearts-hd-2-8-final-chapter-prologue-xbox-release

Not much is known about Halo Infinite yet but it will be another Master Chief campaign which will be avaliable on bothe Xbox and PC. It is also described that it will be more of an open world and the multiplayer will be free.

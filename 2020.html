Top 3 Games of 2020

The Last of Us Part 2 is the sequel made by Naughty Dog. The game takes place 5 years after the event of the first game. Players play as Ellie and Joel. When a tragedy happens Ellie goes on an adventure to gain vengeance.Info https://thelastofus.fandom.com/wiki/ The_Last_of_Us_Part_II#Plot Image from https://www.amazon.com/Last-Us-Gaming-Poster-inches/dp/B07W94TKFH.

Spiderman Miles Morales is the second playstation Spiderman game to be released for the Playstation 4, the first one just being Spiderman. Players play as Miles Morales whose mentor is the original Spiderman Peter Parker. Morales must take over as Spiderman. Infohttps://store.playstation.com/en-us/concept/10000649Image https:// titanbooks.com/70228-marvels-spider-man-miles-morales-the-art-of-the-game/

Call of Duty Modern Warfare's Warzone was perhaps one of the biggest games gamers have seen in a while. Call of Duty decided to make their own Battle Royale gamemode and make it avaliable cross platform on Xbox, playstation, and PC. Tens of thousands of players play this game each day still. Now they have incorporated the new Call of Duty Cold War weapons into Warzone. This game just keeps getting bigger.Imagefrom https://support.activision.com/no/call-of-duty-warzone/articles/all
